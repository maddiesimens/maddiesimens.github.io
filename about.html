To me, thoughtful design is systematic and well-considered—driven by strong opinions, loosely held. It is propelled by curiosity and constantly asking questions. When done right, it feels human.

I’ve spent some time learning to make the Internet a little more human at Wistia and Rdio. As a result, my experience lies in designing both consumer and enterprise products across mobile and web platforms. I’m also into interior design and amateur photography.

This May, I’ll graduate Northeastern with a degree in Business and Interaction Design, which roughly translates to “how to define problems and then solve them.” I’m currently exploring opportunities for a full-time product design position after graduation.

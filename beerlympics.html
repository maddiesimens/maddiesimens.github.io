The Republic of Calumet requests the pleasure of your company and athletic prowess at the XXXI Olympic Games

of

BEER

ABOUT

You’ve been training for years, you just didn’t know it.

Test the fleetness of your wrist and might of your belly at the inaugural olympiad of beer. The games will be held in tournament style, with teams of two partners representing the country of their choice. Teams are expected to dress in honor of their patron nation, and special favor will be granted to the most festive competitors. The tournament victors will be crowned and awarded a prize envied even by the gods.

Come with thirst, leave with glory.

The Story

Dear Friend,

I’ve always liked writing (real, paper + pen) notes to friends like you, but as the years go by, my note-writing has diminished.

The only addresses we exchange these days are email, and randomly texting someone to ask for their address takes the fun out of writing to them out of the blue, just because.

So I figure, if I ask for ALL of your addresses at once, I can write to you all one-by-one.

Hopefully, by the time yours comes, you’ll have forgotten all about this because who doesn’t want to get surprise mail? No one, that’s who.

xoxo,
Maddie

The Details

SHOULD I SIGN UP FOR THIS?

Yes! I’d be delighted to write to best friends, acquaintances, and strangers alike. Feel free to share—I love new friends. The more excuses to write, the merrier.

DO I HAVE TO RESPOND?

There’s no need to reply (unless you really want to), this is a just a fun gesture.

WHAT KIND OF NOTE CAN I EXPECT?

Your note will be handwritten and unique to you.
It might be printed or in cursive.
It might be short and sweet or sprawled across three pages.
It might have a few spelling errosr.
It might come tomorrow or a year from now.
It will certainly be written by me, Maddie Simens.

SHOULD I FEEL WEIRD ABOUT GIVING YOU MY ADDRESS?

Don’t worry, I won’t share your info with anyone or send you prank pizzas.

XOXO, MADDIE
